Overview

Purpose

The purpose of making this website is to give easy acess to the recipes our mother makes. For example, my sister, who is up at college, could pull up the website and easily find any recipe my mom has or added.

Audience

This website is for my family to access and use our recipes where ever we go.

Dynamic elements

I will add a search bar with a bunch of filters, such as a sort by filter, rating filter, time filter, and type of food filter. I will also add the ability to open the recipe when clicking on the small overview on the main page. That way instead of making multiple different pages, I only need one.
